Shuruaat (Appetizers)

Shahi Main Course

  • Nalli Nihari - Slow-cooked mutton shanks in aromatic gravy.
  • Murgh Shahi Korma - Chicken in cashew-saffron sauce.

  • Zafrani Paneer - Cottage cheese in saffron-infused tomato gravy.

Tandoori Riyasat (Tandoor Specials)

  • Sheermal - Saffron-flavored flatbread.
  • Rumali Roti - "Handkerchief" thin bread.

Biryani & Pulao

  • Hyderabadi Gosht Biryani - Mutton layered with fragrant rice.

Meetha (Desserts)

  • Shahi Tukda - Golden fried bread in saffron-cardamom milk.
  • Phirni - Ground rice pudding with pistachio and silver leaf.
